Assigning permissions to a user

You can assign permissions to users by:

  • Assigning roles to users.
  • Selecting individual permissions.

If the user has permissions that were assigned by a user role, those permissions are pre-selected on the Assign Permissions page. You can assign additional permissions as necessary. However, you cannot disable the permissions that were assigned via a user role.

To assign or unassign permissions:

1.         On the Add/Edit Users page, click Assign Permissions. The Assign Permissions page appears, listing all possible permissions.

2.         Select or deselect the appropriate check boxes to assign permissions to or unassign permissions from the user.

3.         Click Save. The changes take effect the next time the user logs in.
