Drug Profile page

In this section ShowHide

On the Drug Profile page, you can view a drug profile. Drug profiles are presentations of graphs and reports related to one or more drugs. From this page, you can select a drug, work with layouts, which are saved collections of charts, and view FDA and NIH label information.

General activities

The following menus appear at the top of the page and affect the entire page:

  • Drug
  • Select
  • Recent

If you click the Manage Layouts link, the Drug Profile Layouts page appears.
