Setting the Home page for a user

  1. Click Admin.
  2. On the left, click Users.
  3. Click a user.
  4. In the Home Page field at the bottom of the page, type the path to the InForm Portal Home page.

    The default path is ./aspmvc/portal/display/renderpage?home=1.

  5. To configure the language for the InForm Portal pages, select the Product Locale and Preferred Study Locale.
  6. Click Submit.
