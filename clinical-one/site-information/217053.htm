Mark a subject as complete

What can't I do after a subject is complete? You can't save any forms that weren't started, or take any other action on a subject, such as screening, randomizing, withdrawing, dispensing, or skipping a visit.

To mark a subject as complete:

  1. On the Home page, determine where to work:
    • To work with real data in Production mode, click the title of the study.
    • To work with mock data in Training mode, click the Training Mode button () on the study.
  2. Along the top, make sure Subjects is selected.
  3. If you have access to multiple sites for the study, select a site from the Site drop-down in the upper-right.
  4. In the table, select the checkbox to the left of the subject you want to mark as complete The subject must have a green avatar with a checkbox in the upper-right corner (), indicating that they completed the minimum required visits in the study.
  5. Above the table of subjects, from the Manage Subjects drop-down, select Complete or Withdraw.
  6. Click Complete Study.
  7. Confirm that you want to proceed, and click Complete Study.

    If a study completion visit opens, complete it now or later.

  8. Answer the questions on the form, and click Complete Study.
