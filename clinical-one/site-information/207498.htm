Enter data and complete a visit

Show me how!

  1. On the Home page, determine where to work:
    • To work with real data in Production mode, click the title of the study.
    • To work with mock data in Training mode, click the Training Mode button () on the study.
  2. Along the top, make sure Subjects is selected.
  3. If you have access to multiple sites for the study, select a site from the Site drop-down in the upper-right.
  4. In the table, locate the subject, and click the visit in the Next Visits column.
  5. If the form contains an editable Visit Start Date question, select today or an earlier date. Depending on how the study is set up, you might be allowed to edit the date after saving the form. The current day is always the latest date that you can select.
  6. Answer the questions on each form. You must answer all required questions, which have a blue asterisk next to the question number.

    If you enter data that doesn't meet the requirements specified by the sponsor, a message explains why the answer does not meet the required criteria. If the question is marked as required, you can't randomize or dispense. If the question isn't marked as required, you can randomize or dispense.

    Tip: On the right, below Question Hint, view details about each question.

  7. Click Save, and then Randomize or Dispense, or click Save & Close to save your changes and randomize or dispense later.

    The button you click to submit the form depends on the type of event associated with the visit. If the visit does not include randomization or dispensation, clicking Save will mark the visit as complete.

  8. In the confirmation window of a dispensation visit, verify the subject number, and note the kit number to dispense.
  9. Click Return to All Subjects to return to the list of subjects at the site, or click Cancel to remain in the visit.

Tips

  • For drop-down questions, you might be able to select more than one option, depending on the study protocol. On the right, below Question Hint, check if there are any instructions about the number of answers you are allowed to select.
  • We recommend that you do not pre-fill information in forms. Instead, start data entry for a form when a visit occurs. If you start a visit for a subject and then your site starts using a new study version, the started visit is unchanged for the subject, even if the new study version has changes to the visit.

What if...

  • I can't click Randomize or Dispense during a visit
  • An error occurs when I try to randomize a subject
  • I started a randomization or dispensation visit before the visit date
  • I randomized a subject in error
  • I can't find the kit Clinical One dispensed to a subject
  • A subject will miss their next visit
  • A subject arrives for a visit outside the visit window
  • I need to transfer a subject
