Update a kit that a subject returned, and reconcile kits

We recommend reconciling kits as the study progresses. For instance, ask subjects to bring any unused investigational product to their next dispensation visit.

You can reconcile kits with the following statuses:

  • Damaged by Subject
  • Dispensed
  • Misallocated
  • Lost by Subject

To do kit reconciliation:

Show me how!

  1. On the Home page, determine where to work:
    • To work with real data in Production mode, click the title of the study.
    • To work with mock data in Training mode, click the Training Mode button () on the study.
  2. Along the top, make sure Subjects is selected.
  3. If you have access to multiple sites for the study, select a site from the Site drop-down in the upper-right.
  4. Select the subject who returned a kit.
  5. On the right in Subject History, click the kit that the subject returned.
  6. From the Kit Status drop-down, select Returned to Site.
  7. Specify the number of returned and missing units and an optional reason, and click Update Kit.
