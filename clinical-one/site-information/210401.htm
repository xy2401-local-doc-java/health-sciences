A subject arrives for a visit outside the visit window

If you're not sure what to do, contact your clinical research associate.

Your next steps typically depend on:

  • Whether the study allows Visits to be completed outside the visit window.

    If the visit window closed before the subject arrives, whether the subject can complete the visit depends on the type of visit and the study protocol. Whenever a visit occurs out of window, Clinical One warns you when you open the visit, and you must confirm that you want to proceed.

    Dispensation visits might be able to occur outside the visit window, depending on the study protocol. If the study protocol does not allow dispensation outside of the visit window, then you cannot dispense a kit to the subject.

    Any visit that does not include a dispensation event can occur outside the visit window in Clinical One.

  • Whether the study allows visits to be skipped.

    Depending on how the study is set up, the skipping of dispensation and non-dispensation visits might be allowed.
