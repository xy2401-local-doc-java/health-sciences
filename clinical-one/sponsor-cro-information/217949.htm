Find incomplete visits

Show me how!

  1. On the Home page, determine where to work:
    • To work with real data in Production mode, click the title of the study.
    • To work with mock data in Training mode, click the Training Mode button () on the study.
  2. Along the top, make sure Subjects is selected.
  3. From the Site drop-down, select a site.
  4. In the table, a yellow visit with an exclamation point in the Previous Visits column indicates an incomplete visit. An orange visit with a question mark indicates a failed screening visit.

Tips

  • Clinical One marks a visit as incomplete if:
    • The visit window passes.

      or

    • A subject arrives within the visit window but the site user doesn't complete the required questions associated with the visit.
  • If the visit is incomplete, then screening, randomization, or dispensation didn't occur.
