Assign blocks of randomization numbers to a site

These steps are for Testing mode. Need to perform this task for a production study?

Do I have to do this? You must assign blocks of randomization numbers to a site if you selected a type of randomization with Fixed in its name, and you either generated a randomization list in Clinical One or uploaded a randomization list that didn't identify sites for blocks.

To assign blocks of randomization numbers to a site:

  1. On the Home page, click the pencil button () on a study, and make sure a study version is in Testing.
  2. Click the Testing Mode button () on the study.
  3. Along the top, click Supplies.
  4. Below the study name, click the Randomizations tab.
  5. If the study has more than one randomization list, select the appropriate list from the Randomizations List drop-down.
  6. To the right of the Randomization List drop-down, click Block Assignment.
  7. For each block number, select a site, and click Save.
