Release kits to depots

These steps are for Production and Training mode. Need to perform this task for a study you need to verify?

Why do I do this? Release kits to a depot to associate the kits with the depot. After kits are released in Production mode, depots can begin distributing. After kits are released in Training mode, users can begin practicing distribution.

To release kits to depots:

Show me how!

  1. On the Home page, click the pencil button () on a study, and make sure a study version is in Approved.
  2. Release kits to depots in Production mode:
    1. Along the top, click Supplies.
    2. Below the study name, click the Inventory tab.
    3. Click a kit type.
    4. Above the kit list, use the filters to return only the kits you want to release to the depot:
      1. Below Location, click Unassigned.
      2. Above the kit list, from the Status drop-down, select Not in Use.
      3. To view kits from the same lot, select a blinded or manufacturing lot from the Lots drop-down.
      4. If necessary, narrow your view further by clicking Kit or Sequence, and enter a range of kit or sequence numbers.
    5. In the list, select the kits to update.
    6. On the right, make sure Kit Settings is expanded.
    7. Below Kit Settings, from the Status drop-down, select Available.
    8. Below Kit Settings, from the Location drop-down, select the depot.

      If you don't see the depot you need, make sure it was created and activated.

    9. Click Update Kits.
    10. In the confirmation window, select a reason for change and click Yes.

      Tip: In the upper right of the kit list, the Total Kits number tells you the number of available kits at the depot.

    11. Above the kit list and to the left of the kit type, click Back, and repeat the previous steps for each kit type.
  3. Release kits to depots in Training mode:
    1. On the Home page, click the Training Mode button () on the study.

      Tip: To return to the Home page, in the upper right, click Home.

    2. Use the previous steps to release kits to depots in Training mode.
