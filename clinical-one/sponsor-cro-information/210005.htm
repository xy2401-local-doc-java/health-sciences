Reveal a subject's treatment arm

Clinical One does not automatically withdraw subjects that have been unblinded with a Code View.

To reveal a subject's treatment arm:

Show me how!

  1. On the Home page, determine where to work:
    • To work with real data in Production mode, click the title of the study.
    • To work with mock data in Training mode, click the Training Mode button () on the study.
  2. Along the top, make sure Subjects is selected.
  3. From the Site drop-down, select a site.
  4. In the table, select the checkbox to the left of the subject you want to unblind.
  5. Above the table of subjects, from the Manage Subjects drop-down, select Code View.
  6. Click Continue.
  7. Confirm the subject number and study name, and select the checkbox to confirm the code break.
  8. Indicate whether an adverse event occurred, and click Continue.
  9. Review the unblinding results for the subject, and click Done.

Tips

  • After you reveal a subject's treatment arm, you can perform the code view as many times as necessary to retrieve the unblinding results again.
