Verify that a site has been activated

Need to activate a site?

  1. On the Home page, click the study settings button () for the study you want to monitor, and select Open Settings.
  2. Below the study name, click the Sites tab.
  3. On the left, select Production Sites or Training Sites.
  4. Select a site.
  5. From the Manage Sites drop-down, select Edit.
  6. Look at the Status field to see if Active is selected.

    Tip: On the Sites tab, a green icon next to the site name means the site has been activated.
