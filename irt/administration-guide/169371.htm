Exporting the audit history

You can export the audit history to save it as a file on your computer.

Audit history reports can get quite large. You might find it useful to filter the log to find specific entries.

  1. Click Admin ().
  2. On the left, click Audit History View.

    For descriptions of the fields on this page, see Audit History View page.

  3. Click Export/Save.
  4. Select the file type, and click Submit.
