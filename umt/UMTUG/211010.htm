Viewing the list of InForm roles

The InForm Roles page lets you view the list of InForm roles and its details. It also lets you filter roles based on the filter criteria.

To view the list of roles:

  1. Select Roles from the menu bar.
  2. Select the sponsor company and trial from the Current Sponsor Company and Trial drop-down lists.

To search for roles:

  1. Enter the filter criteria, and click Find.
  2. To clear the filter criteria, click Clear.
