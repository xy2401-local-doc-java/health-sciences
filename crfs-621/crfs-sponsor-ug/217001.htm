Change users assigned to an InForm rights group

Show me how!

  1. Log into InForm 6.2 as a CRF Submit administrator.
  2. Click Admin.
  3. On the left, click Rights.
  4. Find the rights group and click Change Users.
  5. Add or remove users as necessary.
  6. Click Submit.
  7. Click OK, and then Return.
