Verify the site has downloaded, reviewed, and confirmed the archive or report

How do I do this? Use the Site Confirmation report to show:

  • The site user who confirmed the download and review of an archive or report.
  • The date and time of the confirmation.
  • A cover page includes the sponsor name, study name, and who downloaded the Site Confirmation report and when.
  • For PDF output, the cover page includes the selection criteria applied to the archive request.
  • Submission, Archive, and Custom Archive PDFs include the signature affidavit text.

Show me how!

  1. From the Reports section on the left, select Site Confirmation.

    The user name of the site user who confirmed the download appears in the Confirmed by column.

    Tip: If you don't see the archive or report, it might not have been shared with sites.

  2. Download the Site Confirmation report as a CVS file or a PDF.
  3. The browser prompts you to Open or Save the file. We recommend that you save the report to a local machine.
  4. Click OK.
