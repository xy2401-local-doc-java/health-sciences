InForm training

You are required to take training for each InForm study you will have access to. Depending on the training resources your Sponsor uses, you'll be assigned to Oracle's Health Sciences Learn (HS Learn) training, or to an alternative training resource.

To access HS Learn, you must have an Oracle Account. For more information, see How do I create an Oracle Account for signing in to HS Learn?
